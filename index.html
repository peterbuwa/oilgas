sca technical center

Our technical expertise is centralized, and work is integrated across sca, ensuring strong alignment between technologies we use and our business priorities. This collaboration across value chains helps us address technical challenges and develop solutions that are scalable. We develop and deploy technology to help find and produce new oil and gas reserves, enhance recovery in existing fields, and optimize productivity of downstream assets. We also integrate IT to connect people, processes and ideas.
